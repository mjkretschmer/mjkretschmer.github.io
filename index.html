I am a PhD student in mathematics at the University of Chicago as of Fall 2023, now working with Maryanthe Malliaris. Before that, I was an undegraduate at UC Berkeley. My interests are broadly in logic and algebra, and specifically in the model theory of fields (with order, derivation, etc) and its connections to algebraic geometry and differential algebra.
